# Allen & Heath dLive TCP module

Controls the Allen & Heath dLive.

## Functions:


* Mute Channel
* Mute FX Buses
* Mute Group
* Mute DCA
* Mute Assign
* DCA Assign
* Recall Scene
* Talkback On
* Virtual SoundCheck mode
